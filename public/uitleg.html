Formulas, boundaries and evidence.

Shared assumptions live here so the four main views can remain calm and understandable.

System boundary

One deterministic synthetic household year

No value represents a real person, address, supplier account or connected device.

Energy balance+

Household consumption = grid import + direct solar use.

Solar generation = direct solar use + export.

Battery throughput is shown separately and is not added to consumption, preventing double counting.

What-if calculation+

The calculator varies generation, flexible-load share and storage. Additional captured generation is valued at €0.21/kWh. Purchase, financing, degradation and maintenance costs are excluded.

Every result is hypothetical and bounded to prevent implausible output.

Evidence labels+

Synthetic direct: fictional meter-like input. Inferred: calculated from a balanced identity. Assumed: explicit model input. Forecast: fictional next-day signal.
